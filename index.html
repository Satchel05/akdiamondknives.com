Hello, I’m a young, amature knifemaker who has over a year of experience, and over 20 knives made. Making knives is one of my favorite things to do and I am thankful that I have the time and resources to do it. When I make knives I try to stay focused on all of the details so that I can produce something both functional and beautiful. I strongly value customer satisfaction; it feels uncomfortable on a personal level for me to know that a customer isn’t 100% satisfied with their knife. I am happy to give refunds and/or receive your feedback. NOTE: the top knife is what the knives I sell look like, the bottom ones are ones that I have kept and abused.

The type of knives I have the most experience with are bushcraft, hunting, and general outdoors knives. I am able to makea variety of shapes and sizes of this type of knife. I also make Kitchen Knives, Paring Knives, and EDC knives that are generally for lighter use.

I can’t make knives that have blades more than 8.5 inches long or knives that are wider than 2 inches.

All of my knives are full tang, and I currently only have 1095 steel. Currently I only do hand sanded finishes and vinegar etching. A mirror finish will cost a little more and take longer. If you want acid and stone washing please let me know. I have a neighbor who makes custom molded kydex sheaths. They cost $15, but possibly more for special things like belt clips. I can also have him make sheaths for knives that you may own, but they would have to be shipped to me and back which would add to the price.

My base price is $50 with shipping included. If you want a longer knife, a more time consuming finish, or a knife made with materials I don’t have it may cost more. If you are not satisfied, I will offer a refund for all but $10 (provided you send the knife back). If the knife breaks and you think it’s my fault, let me know and I may fix it or give you a refund.

STEP 1: You can email me a picture of a knife you want made (or just describe what you want) at akdiamondknives@gmail.com.

STEP 2: I will send you a sketch of the knife, and we can tweak it so that you get exactly what you want. I will also ask about what type of blade finish, handle material, etc you want.

STEP 3: I take my payment through PayPal after I have finished and will notify you when it is ready or if there are any issues with the knife.

I am able to restore and fix broken knives. Even if they are not broken I can put a new handle on a knife that has a full tang. If they are expensive or irreplaceable I don’t want to work on them because there is a possibility I could damage or break them. I can also make custom knife blanks and knife making kits which will be in the $30 - $45 range, plus shipping. If you have any questions about knives or knife maintenance email me. I’ll do my best to answer your questions or direct you to more info. I enjoy talking about knives so this is completely free. If you are interested in getting into knife making feel free to reach out. I can give advice on how to start out and not make certain beginner mistakes.
